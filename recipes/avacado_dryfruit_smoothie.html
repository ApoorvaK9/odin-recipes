Avacado-dry fruit smoothie

Description:

Avacado dry fruit smoothie is packed with healthy fats, protein, and fiber, making it a great option for a nutritious breakfast or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy like almond milk or soy milk)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional for extra creaminess)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup mixed dry fruits (almonds, walnuts, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of cinnamon (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh.
  2. Peel and slice the banana.
  3. If your dry fruits are not pre-chopped, chop them into smaller pieces for easier blending.
  4. In a blender, add the avocado flesh, banana slices, milk, and Greek yogurt.
  5. Add the honey or maple syrup for sweetness.
  6. Add the chopped mixed dry fruits.
  7. If using, add the chia seeds or flaxseeds, vanilla extract, and a pinch of cinnamon.
  8. Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  9. If the smoothie is too thick, add more milk a little at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  10. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, you can add a few ice cubes and blend again.
  11. Taste the smoothie and add more honey or maple syrup if needed.
  12. Pour the smoothie into glasses.
  13. You can garnish with a sprinkle of chopped dry fruits or a dash of cinnamon on top if desired.
